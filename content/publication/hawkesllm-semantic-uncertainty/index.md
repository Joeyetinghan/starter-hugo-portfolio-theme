Accepted at the ICML 2026 Workshop on Statistical Frameworks for
Uncertainty in Agentic Systems.

This preprint studies path-dependent uncertainty in agentic text-simulation
systems. HawkesLLM separates temporal influence modeling from text generation:
a multivariate Hawkes process selects compact prompt memory, and a language
model writes each new event from that memory.
