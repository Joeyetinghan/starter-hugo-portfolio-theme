This preprint develops an on-policy decision-focused learning method for
sequential contextual linear optimization with partial feedback.

The approach learns a stochastic predict-then-optimize policy and updates it
with a hybrid gradient estimator combining score-function and decision-focused
plug-in components. Experiments cover top-k selection, shortest path,
combinatorial pricing, and energy scheduling.
