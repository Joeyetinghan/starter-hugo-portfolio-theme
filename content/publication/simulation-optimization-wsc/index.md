This paper is part of **SimOpt**, an open-source testbed of
simulation-optimization algorithms. I contributed test problems such as a
COVID-19 testing frequency problem and an emergency medical volunteer response
problem, and I implemented solvers including STRONG and ADAM. This work
extends that effort by studying solver performance on problems with linear
constraints and by strengthening the benchmark suite available to the
community.
