SimOpt is a testbed of simulation-optimization algorithms. I contributed test
problems such as a COVID-19 testing frequency problem and an emergency medical
volunteer response problem, and I implemented solvers including STRONG and
ADAM. My later work focused on designing solvers that can tackle problems with
linear constraints.
